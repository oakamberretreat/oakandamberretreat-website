Explore The Region

Our location in Sajla keeps you away from the traffic, while still being close enough to Manali's best attractions.

Sajla Waterfall

Just a short, scenic hike from our villa! A hidden gem surrounded by pine trees, perfect for a quiet afternoon.

Mall Road & Old Manali

Drive down to the bustling cafes, local markets, and cultural hubs of Manali when you crave some city energy.

Atal Tunnel & Sissu

Take a day trip through the marvel of Atal Tunnel to witness the completely different, breathtaking landscape of Lahaul Valley.

Solang Valley

The adventure hub! Head here for paragliding, zorbing, ATVs, and skiing during the winter months.
