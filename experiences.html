Villa Experiences

Slow down and soak in the mountain life. Here is what makes a stay at Oak & Amber special.

Bonfire & Stargazing

When the sun dips behind the mountains and the chill sets in, gather around a warm bonfire in our exterior spaces. Enjoy the clear Himalayan night sky, perfect for stargazing and deep conversations with your loved ones.

Home-Cooked Meals

Available on request! Enjoy delicious, hot, home-cooked meals prepared fresh. Whether you are craving local Pahari flavors or comfort food, we ensure you eat well without having to step out of your cozy dining area.

The Ultimate Workation

Tired of working from the city? Bring your laptop to the mountains. With fast Wi-Fi, peaceful surroundings, and inspiring valley views directly from your room, working remotely has never felt this good.
